# Game to build
In this 2D cartoon artillery game, the player selects a unit, drags and releases to aim and fire projectiles at the enemy castle while defending their own destructible base from automated counter-attacks.

# Mechanic name (must appear verbatim in your JS)
drag_and_release_artillery

# Assets (already base64-inlined under const A)
- A.player_castle: The player's destructible base, identified as the blue castle.
- A.enemy_castle: The enemy's destructible base, identified as the red castle.
- A.background: The 2D cartoon environment background.
- A.unit_weapon_1: A selectable unit or weapon from the bottom character selection panel.
- A.unit_weapon_2: Another selectable unit or weapon from the bottom character selection panel.
- A.projectile_1: A unique projectile launched by a unit (e.g., Missile or Tombstone).
- A.projectile_2: A second unique projectile launched by a unit.
- A.background_music: The background music track for the game.
- A.shoot_sfx: Sound effect for dragging, releasing, or projectile impact.

# Required behaviour
- Draw the background, the player castle on the left, and the enemy castle on the right.
- Display a UI at the bottom to select between unit_weapon_1 and unit_weapon_2. Highlight the currently selected unit.
- Display health bars for both castles at the top of the screen.
- The player can drag anywhere on the screen to aim. Draw a trajectory line or aiming vector while dragging.
- On release, spawn the projectile corresponding to the selected unit (projectile_1 for unit 1, projectile_2 for unit 2) from the player's castle, applying physics (gravity) to its trajectory. Play shoot_sfx.
- The enemy castle automatically fires projectiles towards the player's castle at a set interval.
- When a player projectile hits the enemy castle, reduce its health. When an enemy projectile hits the player castle, reduce its health. Projectiles should be removed upon impact.
- If enemy health reaches 0, trigger the Win state and show the CTA. If player health reaches 0, trigger the Loss state and show the CTA.

# Numeric parameters
player_max_health: 100
enemy_max_health: 100
player_damage: 20
enemy_damage: 15
enemy_fire_rate_ms: 2500
gravity: 0.5
max_drag_distance: 150
projectile_speed_multiplier: 0.2

# Win / Loss
- Win: Reduce the enemy (red) castle's health to 0%.
- Loss: The player's (blue) castle's health is reduced to 0%.
