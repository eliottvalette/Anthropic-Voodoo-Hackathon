# 1. About Voodoo

Voodoo is a French tech company that creates mobile games and consumer apps. Founded in Paris in 2013, Voodoo has grown into the biggest French unicorn and one of the leading apps publisher by volume globally.

**Key figures:**

- **$778M revenue in 2025** (+16% YoY), targeting **$1B in 2026**
- **8B+ lifetime downloads**, 150M+ monthly active users, 800**+ employees** across 14 countries

Voodoo started as the pioneer of hyper-casual mobile games (Helix Jump, [Paper.io](http://Paper.io), [Hole.io](http://Hole.io)) and has since shifted to **hybrid casual** — games that are easy to pick up but deep enough to retain players for months. In 2024, Voodoo acquired **BeReal** for €500M, expanding into social apps.

The company operates as both a developer and a publisher, partnering with 100+ studios globally. Voodoo tests ~2,000 prototypes per year and ships only the top performers.

# 4. Technical Setup

## AI

Use AI to its full potential. It's not mandatory to use AI only, but you should push it as far as possible to ship the best product in 30 hours.

## Code

This hackathon is co-organized with **Anthropic**. Every participant gets **free Claude credits** with access to all models (Opus, Sonnet).

**Recommended setup:** [VS Code](https://code.visualstudio.com/) + [Claude Code](https://docs.anthropic.com/en/docs/agents-and-tools/claude-code/overview). **Git** for version control

**Claude Keys:** Check with Unaite how to load the credits

This is what our mentors use and where they can best help you. Not mandatory — use whatever you're fastest with.

## Art

[**Scenario**](https://www.scenario.com/) will be our platform for AI-generated game art (2D and 3D)

**No action required** here - we will give you an access to a Scenario account at the start of the event

**Tips:** stick to **2D art** for faster rendering and easier animation. Use Scenario's style consistency features to keep your visuals cohesive.

## Deliverables

- **Game tracks:** your project must be **playable in a browser**. Use [Three.js](https://threejs.org/) or [Phaser](https://phaser.io/).
- **Non-game tracks:** a working web demo, deployed prototype, or live walkthrough (detailed during April 25th kick-off)
